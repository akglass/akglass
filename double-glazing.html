INSULATED GLASS UNITS (IGU)

THESE ARE ALSO CALLED DOUBLE GLAZING (DGU). 2PENES OF GLASSES OF THE SAME SIZES ARE KEPT APART TO ENCLOSE AIR-SPACE BETWEEN THE PANES. THE BORDERS (OF THE GLASS PANES) ARE CLOSED & SEALED, THUS TRAPPING DRY AIR INSIDE. THIS IS CALLED AN IGU OR DGU. AN IGU IS EFFICIENT IN INSULATING AGAINST HEAT. THUS, AN IGU IN A WINDOW CAN REDUCE SIGNIFICANTLY THE AMOUNT OF HEAT ENTERING THE BUILDING, THEREBY REDUCING THE LOAD ON THE CONDITIONER INSIDE THE ROOM. ALSO, THE ELECTRICITY CONSUMED IN THE AIR-CONDITIONER WOULD BE REDUCED. THUS SAVING YOUR MONEY APART FROM REDUCING THE HEAT ENTRY. IGU CAN REDUCE THE SOUND FROM OUTSIDE TOO. THUS, IF ONE LIVES IN A NOISY PLACE, IGU AN REDUCE THE NOISES. IGU USED FOR WINDOWS, CURTAIN WALLS AND SKY-LITES.

 

THE BENEFITS OF IGU ARE:

REDUSE THE HEAT ENTRY.

REDUCE THE NOISE ENTRY.

MODERN APPEARANCE.
