GLASS TYPES

CLEAR GLASS

CLEAR GLASS IS A COLORLESS  AND WE HAVE A SEVERAL SIZES.

 

4 MM, 6 MM, 8 MM, 10 MM, 12 MM & 15 MM.

 

TINTED GLASS

TINTED GLASS IS ADDITIONS OF COLOR ARE ADDED TO THE GLASS COMPOSITION TO CREATE A TINT, AND WE OFFER  A SEVERAL COLOR.

 

GREEN, GRAY, BRONZE & BLUE.

REFLECTIVE GLASS

REFLECTIVE GLASS IS GLASS THAT HAS BEEN TREATED WITH A METALLIC COATING TO ALLOW IT TO REFLECT HEAT. AND WE OFFER A SEVERAL COLORS.

 

GREEN, GRAY, BRONZE & BLUE.

 

LAMINATED GLASS

MADE FROM TWO OR MORE LAYERS THAT ARE SEPARATED BY AN INTERLAYER OF PLASTIC

PYROBELITE 7 & 12

PYROBELITE IS A LAMINATED GLASS ASSEMBLED WITH ONE CLEAR INTUMESCENT INTERLAYER. IN THE EVENT OF FIRE, THE INTERLAYER EXPANDS AT AROUND 120 ˚C AND TRANSFORMS INTO A RIGID AND OPAQUE FIRE SHIELD, WHICH ENABLES THE GLAZING TO MEET THE INTEGRITY CRITERION OF BS 476: PART 22: 1987 AND TO KEEP THE HEAT RADIATION TO A VERY LOW AND SAFE LEVEL.

FLORA GLASS

PATTERNED GLASS IS A KIND OF DECORATIVE TRANSLUCENT FLAT GLASS WITH EMBOSSED PATTERNS ON ONE OR BOTH SURFACES. WITH THE SPECIAL PROPERTY OF DECORATION, PATTERNED GLASS CAN FULLY ALLOW THE LIGHT TO PASS THROUGH. AND WE OFFER A SEVERAL COLOR.

BLUE, GREEN, YELLOW & WHITE.

MIRRORS TYPES

MIRRORS

A METAL COATING IS APPLIED TO ONE SIDE OF THE GLASS AND SEALED WITH A PROTECTIVE LAYER TO CREATE A MIRROR EFFECT. AND WE OFFER A SEVERAL COLOR.

 

BLUE, GREEN & BRONZE.(6 MM)

CLEAR MIRROR (4 & 6 MM).

 

OUR SERVERS FOR GLASS & MIRRORS

GLASS CUTTING

WE HAVE A DEDICATED GLASS CUTTING SERVICE SO THAT YOU CAN GET GLASS CUT TO SIZE FOR WHATEVER PURPOSE YOU NEED.

TOUGHENED GLASS

ULTIMATE SAFETY GLASS, HIGHLY RESISTANT TO BREAKAGE, WHICH CREATED USING THE THERMAL TEMPERING PROCESS. AND SAFETY AND SECURITY (PANELS STAY TOGETHER WHEN BROKEN).

POLISHING

IT IS A PROCESS TO MAKE THE PARTIES OF THE GLASS OR MIRRORS NOT SHARP.

BEVElING GLASS

IS USUALLY MADE BY TAKING THICK GLASS AND CREATING AN ANGLED SURFACE CUT (BEVEL) AROUND THE ENTIRE PERIPHERY.

SANDBLASTING / ACID-ETCHED

SANDBLASTED GLASS IS PRODUCED BY SPRAYING SAND AT HIGH VELOCITIES OVER THE SURFACE OF THE GLASS.

BACK PAINTED GLASS

BACK PAINTED GLASS IS ANY FORM OF CLEAR GLASS THAT IS PAINTED FROM THE BACK SIDE AND VIEWED FROM THE FRONT SIDE
